Chicken Adobo

A classic Filipino dish with soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Tip: Marinate the chicken for at least 30 mins for better flavor.

How to Cook:

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce, garlic, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and sear until lightly browned.
  4. Pour in vinegar and let it boil without stirring for 3 minutes.
  5. Add water and bay leaves, then simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  6. Adjust taste and serve hot with rice.

Sinigang na Baboy

Sour soup with pork and vegetables, easy for beginners.

Tip: Add tamarind paste gradually to control sourness.

How to Cook:

  1. Boil pork in water with tomato and onion until tender.
  2. Add radish, sitaw (string beans), and eggplant, cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add tamarind paste or sinigang mix to taste.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
